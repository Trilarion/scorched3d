Batteries can be used to heal yourself.
Shields protect your from weapon damage.
Parachutes protect you from falling damage.
Parachutes and shields must be enabled.
Buying fuel will allow your tank to move.
All shots are affected by wind.
The 's' key will show your current score.
The 't' key allows you to talk to players.
The 'a' key will aim the mouse position.
Use the 1 to 9 keys to switch camera views.
The 'c' key will show a second camera view.
